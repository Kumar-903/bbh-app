GitHub Pages Payment Setup
This makes your payment link a tappable blue link in WhatsApp that works for GPay, PhonePe, Paytm and all UPI apps.
Do this once — takes about 5 minutes.
Step 1 — Download the pay.html file from your hostel app folder (provided alongside this file).
Step 2 — Go to github.com and create a free account.
Step 3 — Click New repository. Name it pay. Set it to Public. Click Create.
Step 4 — Click uploading an existing file → upload your pay.html → rename it to index.html → click Commit.
Step 5 — Go to repository Settings → Pages. Under Source select main branch. Click Save.
Step 6 — Your URL will be: https://YOURUSERNAME.github.io/pay
Step 7 — Paste that URL in the WhatsApp tab → GitHub Pages Payment URL field.

Step 8 (For QR on payment page) — If you want your uploaded QR to appear on the payment page:
  a) Save your QR code image as qr.png
  b) Upload it to your GitHub Pay repository alongside index.html
  c) Now upload the same QR in the WhatsApp tab too
The payment page will automatically show your bank QR (no Rs.2000 limit) instead of a generated one.

Backup & Restore
All data is saved in your browser automatically. Export a backup to transfer to another device.
Transfer to phone / another laptop:
1. Click Export Backup — downloads a .json file
2. Send to your phone via WhatsApp, email, or USB
3. Open the app on phone, go to Settings, click Import Backup
4. Select the file — all data restored instantly
